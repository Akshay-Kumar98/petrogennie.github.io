FAQS

  • WHAT WE DO?

    • We deliver Fuel to your doorstep. We want to take away your problems of managing your fuel requirements. We intend to automate the whole process of inventory management in the most reliable, safe, and timely manner.

  • HOW SAFE IS TO ORDER DIESEL AT DOORSTEP?

    • We deliver fuel in state-of-the-art diesel tankers which conform to all safety norms and statutory approvals/licenses/requirements of PESO (Petroleum and Explosive Safety Organization), Weights, Measures, Taxation, etc. Our driver and the co-driver trained and are equipped with all the necessary things to deal with any situation.

  • HOW DO I ENSURE ABOUT THE RIGHT QUALITY & QUANTITY OF THE FUEL?

    • Our fuel are regulated as per the Central / State Government control orders/guidelines. You can ask for on-the-spot certification for both quantity and quality checks. Each delivery vehicle carries relevant equipment to do the checks and certify. Moreover, we procure fuel directly from the oil depots of OMCs (Oil Marketing Companies). The stringent Q&Q checks controlled by these OMCs are being maintained by us, thereby we deliver the same high standard fuel as supplied by OMCs, and hence it is the best quality that we deliver at your doorstep.

  • IS DOORSTEP DELIVERY EXPENSIVE?

    • We charge the same price that you would pay to procure diesel from a petrol pump. However, we do charge a nominal convenience fee for getting it at your doorstep anytime, anywhere.
